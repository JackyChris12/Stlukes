About Us

SLMC Medical Center was set up in 2003 with a vision of providing quality Health Care
Services to corporate organizations and individuals within a cross section, at affordable
costs. The organization was set up in Kisumu to provide distinctive services to the locals and
beyond the Kisumu boundaries.

Vision

To create a society where more people are able to access quality and affordable medical
services through offering high quality medical services to all without any discrimination at
affordable rates.

Mission statement

Strive to provide quality health care to all through proper diagnosis of their ailments, put them on manageable treatment paths, support
them and ensure they achieve their full
recovery at a fair cost.
